Our Team

Our team is comprised of product designers, product managers, software engineers, user experience designers, researchers, and storytellers whose collective work in the civic tech space has helped government agencies and nonprofits deliver better, more equitable services for their constituencies.

We believe in the power of small, cross-functional teams to collaborate and get things done. Our team is nimble, adaptable, and uses Agile practices internally and externally.

Our Approach

We focus on people’s needs to determine how design can help them.

We believe partnerships with people are more valuable than contracts with companies.

We foster a supportive, creative work environment and take a holistic, intentional approach to projects so that we can provide the best solutions possible.

We believe that technology should do no harm. We believe that technology can be designed with people at its center and have positive social impact that, when paired with policy and a critical eye to the challenges that society faces, can improve our way of life. Our intake framework ensures our core values are imbued in every product, practice, partnership, and service we undertake.
